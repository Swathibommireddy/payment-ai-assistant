# Project Vision
## Problem Statement
Customers often experience payment issues such as failed payments, pending transactions, duplicate charges, refund delays, and card declines.
Most customers contact support teams and wait for manual responses.
Support agents spend significant time answering repetitive questions.

## Proposed Solution
Build an AI-powered payment support assistant capable of:
- Understanding customer questions
- Checking payment information
- Explaining payment failures
- Suggesting next steps
- Reading uploaded payment screenshots
- Creating support tickets
- Escalating unresolved issues

## Target Users
- Customers
- Customer Support Agents
- Support Managers
- Administrators

## Expected Benefits
- Faster support
- Reduced operational cost
- Better customer satisfaction
- AI-assisted troubleshooting
- Improved support productivity
